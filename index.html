About Us

The Treadmill Guy is a fitness equipment service company that focuses on repairs and preventative maintenance. We offer a high level of service with quick turnaround time for repairs and an excellent preventative maintenance program in our coverage area. Our goal is to provide the best preventative maintenance program for fitness equipment. Our maintenance program goes beyond the manufacturers suggested maintenance schedule and, with proper care, can therefore increase the lifespan of each piece of equipment we maintain.

We cater to the service needs of fitness equipment owners for both business and consumers alike. The Treadmill Guy is a dynamic company that handles every aspect of fitness equipment service, from preventative maintenance and repair to the even relocating of units.

The Treadmill Guy is in the business of service, and we plan on providing the best service in the area. My mission is to offer the number on preventative maintenance program of fitness equipment and fastest response time for repairs in the areas of Seattle to the Canadian border of Washington. Our philosophy is that of a timely manner, and customer satisfaction and loyalty. Everyone has had experiences with sub par service as well as exceptional service. It is important to us that our customers are completely satisfied with our service and that not only do they become repeat customer but representatives’ of our commitment to excellence!

Our Services

If you have a treadmill, elliptical, stair climber, exercise bike, weight equipment, or any other piece of fitness equipment in need of service, The Treadmill Guy can help you. Please contact The Treadmill Guy with your service need.

Please have the following information ready when you contact us, if possible:

  1. Make and model of equipment.
  2. Serial number of equipment – this will help in getting the correct parts if needed and determine if your equipment is covered under any part or labor warranty.
  3. Date of purchase.
  4. Town the item is located in – we do all work on site.
  5. Brief description of what is wrong.

Warranty Service

The Treadmill Guy is factory authorized to do labor warranty service on many makes and models of fitness equipment. If we can’t help you with the labor warranty, we might be able to help you in finding service for your equipment under labor warranty. If your unit is out of labor warranty – but still covered under part warranty we can assist you in obtaining the warranty parts even if it is a manufacturer not listed above.
